How Do You Sleep?
Sam Smith

"How Do You Sleep?" is a song by English singer Sam Smith, released on 19 July 2019. Smith co-wrote the song with Savan Kotecha, Max Martin and Ilya, the latter of whom produced the song.

Critical reception

Rolling Stone described the song as a "pop ballad about trying to move on".

Music video

The music video was released alongside the song on 19 July. It was directed by Grant Singer, with choreography by Parris Goebel, and features Smith dancing with a troupe of shirtless male dancers wearing waist trainers.

Promotion

Smith posted on their social media accounts on 9 July that they had an announcement to make the following day. The pre-order link became active on 10 July, and later the same day, Smith formally announced and shared a clip of the song. The song is featured on Now That's What I Call Music!
